The Butterfly Effect



I blinked and
The Sand in the Clock shifted
Kings went bang
The clouds were lifted
Moons blossomed
All the people twitched
The branches ticked
As if they itched
The waves all melted
The sunset swirled
The cheetahs leaped
The grasses twirled
