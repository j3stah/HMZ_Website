Subconscious


Cool, soft shadows
Velvet darkness
A dark sleepy desert
The bottom of a deep, thoughtful ocean
I just want to rest
I just want to sleep


Rippling caustics play silently
The blankets of the earth hold me close
Protect me from tomorrow
I’m scared of tomorrow


Violin and a setting sun
The sea begins to darken
I’m walking home across the grass
The music soars
The waves begin to rise
I sink to my knees and look out to where the sea glows
A world that made no sense
And such a strange story
and it’s nearing its end


Out to the sun, he swims
Alone in the fading light
Far from the island
A speck against the water
No one understands how close he is to home
Almost to the end where no one has gone
Tears of joy mix with the shining water
