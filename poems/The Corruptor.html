The Corruptor


His colored glasses shine as he grins with hate
Their rules forbid him from reasoning with them
Nobody in charge makes the rules, the ones who do are far
Too far it’s gone, it’s come| to this. He slowly revs the car


The bloody sun makes the horizon quake with fear
The car a blistered, beat-up bullet goes diving up a hill

His friends all want to use the system, the system takes too long
He wants to burn it all and start again, the limitations gone
They fear the chaos he fears the pass-age of time
They resolve to save a rotting tree, he runs away-from the grime
