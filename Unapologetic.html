Unapologetic


For just this once I’m unapologetic
If you’re feeling apopletic
Then go get it
You can tell them all I said it
That I did it
That I spit it
That I maxed out every digit


Because I’m coping
By not choking
Forget my life and all my hoping


Because I’m right now
And I know how
this ends because I’ve seen it
and I’ve been it
and I’ll lean it to you all
like a brick wall
Crashing down and avalanching
Kind of how my power’s branching
just like lightning
And it’s frightening
All this moral wrong and rightening.
