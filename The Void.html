The Void


The void is neat and ordered
It's bordered
by impossible contours
Blank stares turn to statues
concentric eyes cannot track you
Nothing is a trinket
So I pour the essence of nothing
into a glass and drink it
Gray air morphs into
Gray stares
My head is dizzy and aching
Throw up static, painstaking
My 1's turn to 0's
That line up in neat rows
of nothing, while
the ghost of a shell peers out at you
But glazed eyes cannot track you
from inside of a vacuum where
Rigid black gridlines
turn into landmines
You never looked at the system
to see if it fits him
You take his words and turn them
into Lorem Ipsum
The pathways all curve but go to the same place
The wind curves and it erodes at my face.
