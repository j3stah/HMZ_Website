The Dwarves


Up in the sky, thereâ€™s nothing there
Naught but clouds and gas and air
We down below, better fare
The Dwarves of Solid Stone


Here at the Core we stoutly stand
Vast and glittering is our land
Of Magma is our burning brand
The Dwarves of Emerald Throne


Smoothly hewn by water and time
The clinks and clanks of picks will rhyme
Herbs will burn in moss and lime
The Dwarves of Rocky Bone


Shimmering, Glimmering, Glowing Flowing
Down, down to the bottom going
Deep dark seas with lanterns, rowing
The Dwarves have what has Shone


Marble Cities, Turquoise Arches
Over ravines and cracks Dwarf marches
Opal Forts and Bismuth bridges
The Dwarves of Gong and Tone


Gold and Geodes up on the wall
Veins of Silver streak the hall
Some is chiseled but not all
The Dwarves love what they Own


Strike with hammer, swing with pick
Our heritage is rich and thick
Beards of moss and hearts of brick
The Dwarves have never Flown


Anchored as Welded Metal
Firmly shining Diamond Petal
Smartly sink and slowly settle
The Dwarves have always Grown


Hearty stew, Axes and staffs
Solemn chanting, deep raucous laughs
Instinct, knowledge, no parchment graphs
The Dwarves can be Alone


Clan, hearth, beard, fire
Forge, stone, gem, sire
Armor, hammer, axe, pyre
A lot, The Dwarves, have known
