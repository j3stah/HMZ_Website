An Armchair

A brownish-red armchair sits in the corner
It leans heavily, comfortably into the floor
A great, sleepy bear come to rest
A wise, rumbling boulder sunken into place
A bearded professor snoring away
A dark-timbered barrel filled with rich syrup
A gem-crudded throne with a film of dust
A silky cat showering in the sun
A slow ocean wave of peace and comfort
