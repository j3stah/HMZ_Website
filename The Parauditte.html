The Parauditte


The giant, ancient, Forest Rulers
Put on new robes with glee
And paraded down the golden shore
To the Monarchs of the Sea


A white-crested wave
A blossoming smile


They sat down to talk for a little while


The stars marched by
As the sun grew old


Many a tale and story were told
Until the sea was shimmery gold


Time to go
Good-bye
