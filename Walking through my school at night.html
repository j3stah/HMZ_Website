Walking through my school at night
Face resigned as the lamps glow bright
The halls are dark and the classrooms sealed
At this my sense of ease revealed


I cannot be harmed for now,
That, the locked doors will not allow
I no longer mill around with dread
The thought of failure leaves my head


But there are figures in the halls
I see their shadows on the walls
Their backs are bent, bent from more
Than anything their packs could store
